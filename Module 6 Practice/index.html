Welcome to PagleWaters

If you are tired of your daily routine and want to take a rest in a quiet place alone with yourself or spend time with your family – fishing in PagleWaters Resort is the right choice. Our resort represents the best fishing destination with a diverse variety of fish species. With our range of boats and professional angling guides, you can enjoy a unique fishing experience.

About Our Club & Resort

The PagleWaters Fishing Club & Resort was established in the early 1980s. It is the premier destination for freshwater and saltwater fishing, a home for a variety of fish species.

Here, you can find exotic fish species all year round. We offer 4 different fishing adventure packages for any group size. Our high-quality boats will give you the freedom of movement you deserve. Enjoy the comfort of our private lodges surrounded by conifers and treat yourself to a gourmet dining experience at the end of a long fishing day.

Our Resort

Dining

Our kitchen is at your service all day long up to midnight. Be you a meat lover, seafood fan, or even a vegetarian, our professional team of cooks offers a diverse menu and is ready to satisfy any taste buds.

Lodging

We offer a variety of luxury lodging selections ranging from one to five bedrooms. They are equipped with kitchen utensils, bed linen, LED TVs, first-class home entertainment facilities, and free Wi-Fi covering the entire resort.

The Clubhouse

Our luxury resort is surrounded by dozens of islands which are national parks with pristine nature, a forest paradise full of wildlife. Our clubhouse is at the center of this amazing beauty.

Gallery

No matter what type of fishing you prefer, PagleWaters Resort is a wonderful place to spend time with your best friends.

Membership

We allows our club members to go fishing and unwind whenever they like at no extra cost by only calling in advance to make a reservation. You can also fish with a rented lodge or camp at a small day fishing fee. The membership also gives members a 15% discount on any lodge rental when they want to stay for several days.
