Welcome to PagleWaters

If you are tired of your daily routine and want to take a rest in a quiet place alone with yourself or spend time with your family – fishing in PagleWaters Resort is the right choice. Our resort represents the best fishing destination with a diverse variety of fish species. With our range of boats and professional angling guides, you can enjoy a unique fishing experience.

Activities

We deliver all kind of services that support small and micro businesses.

First-Class Fishing

You can fish on your own or with our professional angling guides. We’ll do our best to offer you the fishing journey experience of a lifetime!

Picturesque Views

Our luxury resort is surrounded by pristine nature. Here, you have an opportunity to spend time in fresh air or immerse inside the beauty around you.

Beaches

Our resort has two beaches where you can have hours of pleasure with the whole family. You can play football or volleyball, or just savor a great evening by the campfire.

Boat & Motor Rental

Staying at our resort you can experience the ocean yourself with our boat renting service. Our diverse fleet consists of boats that can meet any taste and budget.

Dinners

PagleWaters Resort provides its guests with the exceptional dining experience along with spectacular nature views.

Bar

PagleWaters Resort offers a full-service bar with a wide range of drinks to celebrate your long fishing adventure.
